Fe Nix Ranks are awarded based on account type, time in our clan, progress, and achievements.

If your only remaining requirement is time in the clan, then your rank will be updated at the end of a month. Otherwise, reach out to a mod to get your rank updated.

Each rank builds on the previous, so you need to achieve everything from the lower ranks before getting the next rank.

Regular Accounts

Air Rank

Less than 1 Month in the Clan

Water Rank

1 Month in Clan

Earth Rank

All F2P quests and either 50 EHP or 25 Million XP

Fire Rank

1 Boss Unique Drop, 300 Clue Caskets, and either 250 EHP or 125 Million XP

Nature Rank

2 Boss Unique Drops, 600 Clue Caskets, and either 550 EHP or 250 Million XP

Death Rank

Complete F2P Collection Log except Stale Baguette, 100 Obor Chests, 100 Bryophyta Chests, and either Maxed Total Level, 1500 EHP, or 500 Million XP
